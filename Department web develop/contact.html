How to Reach Us

The School of Mathematical Sciences is located in the Science Complex on the North Maharashtra University campus. Visitors can enter through the main university gate and follow the signs to the Science Complex.

View on Map

Find Us

Directions

From Jalgaon Railway Station

The university is approximately 8 km from Jalgaon Railway Station. Auto-rickshaws and taxis are available outside the station.

From Bus Stand

Regular city buses operate from Jalgaon Bus Stand to the university campus. The journey takes approximately 20 minutes.

Parking

Visitor parking is available near the Science Complex. Please collect a visitor pass from the security gate.

Frequently Asked Questions

The administrative office is open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The office is closed on Sundays and public holidays.

You can schedule a meeting with faculty members by contacting them directly via email or by reaching out to the department office. Faculty members typically have office hours when they are available to meet with students and visitors.

For admission inquiries, please contact our admissions office at admissions.maths@nmu.ac.in or call +91 257 2258429. You can also visit our website's admission section for detailed information about application procedures, eligibility criteria, and important dates.

Yes, we offer campus tours for prospective students and their families. Tours are typically conducted on weekdays and must be scheduled in advance. Please contact the administrative office at admin.maths@nmu.ac.in or call +91 257 2258431 to arrange a tour.

Access to research facilities is available to enrolled students, faculty members, and approved research collaborators. External researchers interested in using our facilities should contact the Research Coordinator at research.maths@nmu.ac.in to discuss collaboration opportunities and access procedures.
